Jump Squid

  Jump Squid is a small squishy squid creature with ranged up close moves with his tentacles, and variety of moves.
  Squid is great at maintaining stage control. His ink slows down enemy opponents making any dash attacks on their part easily punishable. His long range swinging tentacles help him with guarding against recovering or airborne opponents.
  His tentacle whip can rack up lots of quick damage and combos easily with ink jet followed by an up strong attack. When the opponent is airborne, follow this and juggle them in the air wiht up-aerials. Ink jet is also useful for gimping the recoveries of oppoents, although super-armor powers through it.
