Sword King

  Sword King is a strong, solid, and balanced character, yet with useful projectiles which promote his strong zoning game. He is effective at play both offensively and defensively. Using his long sword and swirling Up-B, he is able to manage the immediate space around him very effecively. His shield out of shield is also very useful because it can block tons of projectiles.
  His bow and boomerang have decent to spectacular range depending on the amount of time spent charging the attack. They can be useful in edgeguarding - even being used from across the stage - and breaking enemy combos. Sword King is a great team player in doubles, with strong moves to protect teammates and ranged attacks to protect their teamates from edgeguarding or combos by opponents.
  His Up-B Spin Attack, and has a decent recovery range. It can KO opponents midair at about 120% or higher damage. On the ground, it is a powerful attack that will deal a good amount of damage to opponents and launch them a good distance as well. This attack is great for spacing opponents and sending them far distances when the approach too close.
